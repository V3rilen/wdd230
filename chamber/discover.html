FilmQuest
FilmQuest is a film festival centered around the Fantastic, and celebrates the best independent sci-fi, fantasy, and horror cinema in the world over 9 days of screenings, parties, events, workshops, panels and more in Utah each fall.
Pumpkins in The Orchard
Come see the Pumpkins located in The Orchard at University Place. The display consists of hundreds of pumpkins and will be up from October 2nd - 31st. Come during the day or at night. The pumpkins will be lit from about 5:00 - 9:00 pm. See if you can find our 6 hidden special pumpkins highlighting things in Utah.
Van Gogh to Playdough
Join us this October as we look and observe Gabriel Dawe's Plexus! Together we will talk about thread, spiders, and how small things can make great things! Students will then get to make their own spooky spider web to take home!
